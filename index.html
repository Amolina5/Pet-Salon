Welcome to The Fashion Pet!

Our pet grooming salon is your one-stop shop for all your pet care needs. We provide a vast range of grooming services to ensure your pets look and feel their best. Our friendly staff is highly trained and experienced, providing unparalleled care to all pets that visit our salon..

Grooming

Pet Grooming is an essential part of keeping pets healthy and happy. A good grooming routine helps prevent skin infections, keeps pets clean and eliminates parasites. Fashion Pet provides state-of-the-art grooming services that cater to the specific needs of individual pets. Their professional staff is equipped with the latest grooming technology and techniques, which ensure that pets always look their best. The grooming process includes a custom-bath with top-quality pet-safe shampoos and conditioners, nail clipping and filing, ear cleaning, hair brushing and trimming, and teeth brushing. Fashion Pet's commitment to using the best grooming techniques and top-quality products ensures that pets receive the care they deserve. Their services leave pets feeling refreshed, comfortable and looking fantastic..

Health Checkups

When it comes to pet checkups, Fashion Pet makes sure that pets receive the best possible care. Their approach involves using the latest technology and techniques to diagnose and treat your pet's health issues. They start by conducting a thorough examination to uncover any possible issues. The process is easy, convenient and stress-free for pets, as the experienced veterinarians and staff are skilled at handling pets with care. Fashion Pet's commitment to providing the best pet checkup experience ensures that pets remain healthy and happy for years to come..
